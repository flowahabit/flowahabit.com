Your data, your control

No Server Account

Flowa does not create or maintain accounts on our servers. All your habit data, streaks, and settings are stored locally on your device only.

Signing in with Google is optional and only used to identify your Pro subscription — we do not store any personal data on our end.

How to Delete Your Data

  1. Uninstall the app — this permanently removes all locally stored habit data, streaks, and settings from your device.
  2. Revoke Google access — visit myaccount.google.com/permissions, find Flowa, and remove access.
  3. Cancel Pro subscription (if active) — go to Google Play → Subscriptions → Flowa → Cancel.

Request Data Deletion

If you have any concerns or would like to confirm that no data is held about you, contact us and we will respond within 7 days.
