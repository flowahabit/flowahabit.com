Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

Flowa ("we", "our", or "us"), developed by Brightline Studio, is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

Habit & app data: All habits, logs, streaks, and settings you create are stored locally on your device. We do not collect or store this data on our servers.

Google account (optional): If you choose to sign in with Google, we access your name and email address solely to identify your Google Drive backup folder. We do not store your Google credentials.

Google Drive backup (optional): If you enable backup, your habit data is saved to your personal Google Drive. We have no access to this data.

AI features (optional): If you use AI habit suggestions or progress summaries, your habit names and basic stats are sent to Google's Gemini API to generate responses. No personally identifiable information is included.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect analytics or crash reports
  • We do not serve ads or share data with advertisers
  • We do not sell your data to any third party
  • We do not collect your location

3. In-App Purchases

Purchases are handled entirely by Google Play. We do not process or store any payment information.

4. Children's Privacy

Flowa is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@flowahabit.com.
